Legal & Litigation Appraisals

When value will be reviewed, challenged, or relied on in a legal context, you need a report that is clear, transparent, and defensible. We provide appraisal support for lawyers, courts, and private parties across BC.

Common Legal Uses

Divorce & separation
Estate disputes
Retrospective valuations
Mediation support
Appraisal review

BC Assessment values are often referenced, but they are mass-appraisal figures and may not reflect true market value for a specific property or valuation date. A professional appraisal provides more reliable, defensible support.

What You Get

Defensible methodology: clear comparable selection and market analysis.

Clear writing: understandable reports for legal audiences and non-technical readers.

Support: appraisal review capability and expert witness support where required.

Typical turnaround: 1–2 business days after inspection (scope-dependent).
